How it works

Built into the house, not bolted onto it.

01

Set in the wall

The Blends backing mounts flush inside the wall cavity during construction — no cabinet, no protruding box, no visible footprint in the room.

02

Skim-coated in plaster

A thin layer of plaster or joint compound is applied directly over the speaker face, blending it seamlessly into the surrounding wall or ceiling.

03

Painted to vanish

Painted over with the rest of the room. Sound passes straight through the finish — so the wall plays music, and the speaker is invisible. Forever.

The experience

Sound from everywhere. Speakers from nowhere.

Walk into the room and it's simply filled with music — warm, even, and effortless — with nothing on the walls to tell you where it's coming from. No black grilles interrupting the millwork, no boxes competing with the architecture. Just the home, sounding the way it should.

Why this, for this house

A two-hundred-year-old home deserves technology it can't see.

Every other speaker asks the house to make room for it. Blends asks nothing. The original plaster, the millwork, the oxblood doors and columned porches stay exactly as they are — and the music lives invisibly inside them. For a home being restored with this much care, it is the only kind of speaker worth installing.
